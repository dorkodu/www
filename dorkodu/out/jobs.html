How We Work?

First know, then trust the process.
  • Highly Aligned, Loosely Coupled.
    Members of our team understand high-level company goals, are autonomous, and show initiative.
  • Ambition Shapes Reality.
    We are extremely ambitious in what we can accomplish. We set high standards, expecting to fail in the short-term but knowing that failure guides us to learn and ultimately succeed.
  • Product-Driven.
    We invest in a world-class experience that reduces massive friction for our users and speaks for itself.
  • Asynchronous Productivity.
    We are a globally distributed and diverse team that values deep, asynchronous work, effective and clear communication.
  • Learn Through Building
    It's time to build! We ship frequently, connect with our community, and iterate until we get it perfect.

Our Story

A timeline of pursuing meaning in technology.
1
Our founders, Doruk and Berk are born in 2004.
Both always wanted to be inventors, Doruk was more of an artistic kid. Berk was a child chess prodigy.
2
Doruk and Berk learn to code at 10-11 years old.
Finally found their passions. Computers!
3
Doruk and Berk entered Vefa Lisesi in 2018. When they met, instant best friends!
Vefa is a historical high school in Istanbul.
4
Doruk and Berk starts working together.
Berk starts building games, after being an international e-sports wonderkid.
5
Dorkodu's seeds are planted during 2019, when the duo brand their all software under one name.
"Dorkodu" = Doruk + Berk + Code.
6
Doruk realizes how they can mix his design ideals with their revolutionary technology to make the dream of utopian human life possible.
Now they found their purpose.
7
Dorkodu becomes their full-time hustle.
Now school is the hobby.
8
Dorkodu wins 1st place in a national software contest in 2020.
Dorkodia, an experimental social app for meditation & productivity.
9
Doruk and Berk publish many open source projects.
Complex software libraries, protocols and frameworks; even pixelart.
10
Aziz and Furkan join the Dorkodu revolution as our first rookies.
First greenpills escaped the Matrix.
